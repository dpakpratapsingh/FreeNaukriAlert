# P3c: Practice Engine Strategy

The practice engine provides actionable data to students by serving past and predicted question papers.

## Structural Arrangement
All papers must be systematically tagged and arranged:
1. **By Year** (e.g., 2022, 2023, 2024)
2. **By Shift** (e.g., Morning Shift, Evening Shift)

## Content Pipeline
- **Previous Papers**: Digitized exact replicas of past government exams.
- **Predicted Papers**: AI-generated mocks based on the "Examiner Thought Process" algorithm.
- **Feed Papers**: Ongoing, continuous practice questions fed to users based on their target routine.
