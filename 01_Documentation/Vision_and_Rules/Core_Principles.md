# Core Principles: Free Naukri Alert

## Non-Profit Publication
Motive: To serve Competitive Students, with future expansions targeting Government Employees for job upgrades, and international branches.

## Judging Boundary (Strict)
The Platform and AI engine will **ONLY** judge a student's **Eligibility**.
Under no circumstances is the platform allowed to judge or score a user based on:
- Personality
- Actions
- Emotion

## Reward & Notification Loop
- **Student Flow**: Registration -> Assessment -> Continuous alerts (Job, Exam, Result, Syllabus, Routine).
- **Contributor Flow**: Once successful, students exit as Role Models / Contributors and are rewarded for life exploration contributions.
