# Workflows & Mechanics

## 1. Onboarding
- Manual registration to determine student requirement and manifestation goals.
- Profiling of short past (Educational Eligibility, Choices, Target).

## 2. Assessment Engine (The 100-Question Limit)
The system calculates an **Activity Percentage Score** via the following algorithmic checks:
- **Syllabus Check**: Extracts the 5 most repetitive questions from every topic in the syllabus.
- **Examiner Thought Process Check**: Analyzes questions outside the syllabus to evaluate true readiness and logic, capped at a 100-question limit.
